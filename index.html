Per-leg strategy

Top-of-mind

  • Run the runnable bits. AS1→AS2 at 10:30/km — that's your flat-30k Z2 pace with race-day buffer. Don't hike sections you'd run training.
  • Power-hike the steep bits. AS3→AS4 at 27:00/km is recon-validated; chasing VP here = quad blowout by AS5.
  • AS4 cutoff 15:00 — v4 arrives at 13:48 with 1h 12m cushion. Comfortable but earn it on AS1→AS3.
  • Heat hits 12:00–15:00 = exactly your AS3→AS4 leg. Drench buff, drink Pocari at every AS.
  • Caffeine starts at AS3 (Coca Cola). Sari Roti every 30 min throughout.

Hourly targets

The simple rule

Every 30 min: 1× Sari Roti Cokelat + sip Pocari.

Every 60 min: 1× salt cap + force a full drink.

Every AS: drain & refill both flasks with Pocari Sweat.

AS3 + AS4: sit, eat banana + rice/soto + Coca Cola.

Caffeine: Coca Cola at AS3 onwards (AS3, AS4, AS6, AS7). Backup gels only.
